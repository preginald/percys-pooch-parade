Meet Percy

Your neighbourhood dog walker

I'm Perseus — but everyone calls me Percy. I live right here in Ivanhoe and I've loved dogs for as long as I can remember. When I'm not at school, you'll find me at the park with a four-legged friend (or three!).

I started Percy's Pooch Parade because I believe every dog deserves an awesome walk with someone who truly cares. I treat every pooch like they're my own — with patience, energy, and plenty of treats (with your permission, of course!).
